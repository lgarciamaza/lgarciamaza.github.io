Research Intership - Universidad del Atlántico

Call for the strengthening of projects in execution of CTeI in health sciences with young talent and regional impact improving Scientific and Technological Capacities for the Diagnosis of SARS Cov-2/COVID-19 and Studies of Biological Agents of High Risk to Human Health in the Department of Atlántico. The objectives of the training and research activities were the social appropriation of knowledge in the area of antimicrobial resistance, in order to disseminate advances, socialize strategies and raise awareness about the processes of resistance to antibiotic agents.

Achievements during this project:

  • Obtaining a series of tetrahydroquinoline derivatives with activity on bacterial Quorum Sensing to help create research bases to combat the problem of resistance to multiple drugs in the department of Atlántico

  • To raise the awareness of the target population about the problems identified and action measures through talks with the community of the population in the area of influence of the Universidad del Atlántico (Urbanization of La Playa and Las Florez) on the factors associated with the processes of antimicrobial resistance.

  • Preparation of an information booklet focused on the problem of antimicrobial resistance. It can be found here .

  • Article disseminating the results of the research project in obtaining agents with possible biological activity.

During the development of the proposal I became qualified in microbiological techniques, which allowed me to study and evaluate the antimicrobial activity of compounds (auto-inductors) with possible biological activity (antimicrobial activity). In addition, to identify phenotypic changes in the study of autoinductors. On the other hand, the internship allowed me to develop skills such as Organic Chemistry at the Laboratorio de Investigación de Biología Molecular - Universidad del Atlántico (LIBM-UA); in synthetic processes for obtaining molecules with possible antimicrobial activity, and interpretation of spectra of isolated compounds through IR, EM and NMR techniques (1H, 13C, DEPT, HMBC and HSQC). Additionally, I have a bit of skills in bioinformatics techniques for the study of proteins (Proteomics): Applications in Biomedical Research, Development and Diagnosis. This allowed me to complete my integral training for the good development of theoretical-practical activities within the main proposal of the project and collaboration in the required areas.
