Research Project

My last year as a science undergraduate student was in the area of Chemical biology, more specifically in the development of bioactive compounds to combat the problem of antibiotic resistance. This project is seen as having a positive impact not only within the scientific community but also on the vulnerable population facing the global emergence of multidrug resistance . Research project with impact on the community

During the development of the research project, I was selected within Convocatoria para el fortalecimiento de proyectos en ejecución de CTeI en ciencias de la salud con talento joven e impacto regional , which allowed me to take my work to a higher level. In this case, the research had a positive impact not only on academia but also within the community. Here, through the fellowship as a young researcher, I had the opportunity to strengthen the knowledge of vulnerable populations and strengthen the ties of cooperation and participation with the community in order to consolidate the university-society relationship , making it possible to raise awareness and sensitize the population with risk factors about diseases caused by biological agents of epidemiological alert, more specifically in the issue of antibiotic resistance.
