Caribe Microbial Meeting 2022

The CaMM (Caribe Microbial Meeting) is a space for scientific dissemination for young researchers whose purpose is to publicize the research that is being carried out in the Caribbean region in the area of ​​Microbiology, and in this way create or strengthen research networks that allow strengthening and positioning research in the Colombian Caribbean region.

Type of event: National Congress

Type of participation: Poster

Title: Efecto de una serie de derivados o-tolil(1,2,3,4-tetrahidroquinolin-4-il)formamida sobre el crecimiento bacteriano y formación de Biofilm en Pseudomonas aeruginosa

Place: Valledupar, Cesar - Universidad Popular del Cesar, 28/10/2022.

II Congreso de Química del Caribe, International Meeting

The CQC (Congreso de Química del Caribe) is an event organized by Grupo de Investigación en Ciencia, Educación y Tecnología (CETIC) and the Chemistry Program of the Faculty of Basic Sciences of the Universidad del Atlántico. The II Caribbean Chemistry Congress, under the slogan "Chemistry Without Borders", brings together students and professionals from Chemistry and related areas, Chemistry and Pharmacy, Biology, Engineering, Materials, among others, to discuss the latest innovations and chemistry applications. In this space, the experiences of the use of the concepts and techniques of chemistry in different areas of knowledge will be shared, for the development of their scientific work, highlighting its character as a central science.

Type of event: National Congress

Type of participation: Assistant

Place: Barranquilla, Atlántico - Universidad del Atlántico, 27/02/2019 - 01/03/2019.
